Cheese Cake

This is our new simple new york cheese cake.

About ingreddians

  • 500g of cake flour
  • 9 eggs
  • 750g castor sugar
  • 3kg cream cheese
  • 150g butter
  • 50mls vanilla essence
  • 2packets of tennis biscuits
    • half caramel on top of cheese cake

Methods

      Mix Cream cheese, Vanilla essence and Sugar together
      Add the eggs one at a time and lastly fold in your flour
      For base: 2 packets of tennis biscuits crushed mixed with 150g butter
      Line spring form cake tin with wax paper
      Add crushed biscuits and compact it
      Pour mixture over and bake at 180'C for 10mins then turn temp down to 150'C
      Turn off your oven and let it cool down over night
