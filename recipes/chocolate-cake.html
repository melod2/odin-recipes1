Chocolate Cake

The most wanted cake for lovers

  • 450g cake flour
  • 3g baking powder
  • 3g salt
  • 645g sugar
  • 6 eggs
  • 15ml vanilla essence
  • 15g bicarb
  • 1 cup black coffee
  • 375 oil
  • 150g cocoa powder
  • 625ml butter milk

Icing

      hot milk

Methods

Heat oven 180'C and line two cake tins. Mix all dry ingredients together.Cake flour,baking powder,cocoa powder, bicarb and salt. Mix all wet ingredients together, Eggs, Oil,Water, Vanilla essence and Butter milk Add wet ingrediants and dry ingrediants together mix well. Pour into cake tins and put it into the oven Bake for 60 minutes
