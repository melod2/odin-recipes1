Suvory Muffin

Try our simple suvory muffin recipe

  • 250g cake flour
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp baking powder
  • 80ml oil
  • 250ml milk
  • 200g cheddar
  • 150g feta
  • 150ml buttermilk
      Salt
      Pepper

Method

Mix all your wet ingrediants. Mix all your dry ingrediants in a separate bowl. Put Wet and dry mixture together and add your feta, and cheddar. Bake at 180'C for 30min or until baked through
